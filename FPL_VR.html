VIRTUAL REALITY CONDITION ASSESSMENT TRAINING APPLICATION

The Florida Power & Light Company wants a virtual reality training software that will assist in training their employees. This software will specifically be used to teach employees how to:

  • Identify various overhead equipment on the poles
  • Determine if there is damage to equipment
  • Rate the damage on the equipment on a five point scale

The Florida Power & Light Company also wants the ability to quiz their employees to see if they can correctly identify equipment and rate the damages on the equipment using a five point scale.

Specifications/Requirements

Upon starting the application, the trainee will have the option to:

  • Target their training: For example, the trainee may choose to see examples of equipment with level 4 damages
  • Train from a random selection: This will show the trainee random examples of equipment and damages
  • Take a quiz from a random selection: If the trainee scores less than a 70 on this quiz, they will be prompted to retake the quiz
  • Take a quiz from a targeted selection:

In all of these options, the trainee will be walking around the poles from the ground.
